This is a live recorded video that explains a bit the basics of what it means to make images with mathematics. I am trying to talk comprehensively in English about what I am doing while coding, which makes things slower and less clear than they should, but that's all I got time for this time.

The video was live recorded with VLC, which I didn't know wouldn't capture the mouse cursor, so sometimes I am talking about things that I am pointing at with the mouse that we cannot see in the video. My bad. I know for next time.

The coding happened in Shadertoy.com, and the final code/result is publicly available here:

https://www.shadertoy.com/view/XssSRX

Enjoy!
